Observable/Observer

Observable belongs to the rxjs package, but Angular heavily depends on it.

Fibonacci Observable

In this demo, you can subscribe to a FibonacciObservable. It will show you the Fibonacci numbers. You can subscribe multiple times if you want to you. Just check out the console to see the actual numbers.

Notice what happens when you subscribe and then move away from the page. Your subscription is still active, but you can't unsubscribe anymore. Therefore it is good practice to remove your subscriptions in the onDestroy() method.

Subject

Be sure to check-out the Subject component. It is both Observer and Observable. Use it in your code instead of the event emitter.
